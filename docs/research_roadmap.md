# Research Roadmap

This roadmap is for a **research prototype** exploring AI-assisted skin lesion risk screening.

## Phase 1: Public dataset baseline (MVP)

- Use public datasets (HAM10000, ISIC)
- Establish baseline models and data pipelines
- Define label mapping (binary vs multiclass)
- Implement robust evaluation and documentation

## Phase 2: Model comparison & iteration

- Compare architectures (ResNet50, EfficientNet variants)
- Explore data augmentation and class imbalance strategies
- Calibration and uncertainty estimation (optional)
- Explainability experiments (Grad-CAM) for research insights

## Phase 3: Research/clinical collaboration (only with oversight)

- Seek institutional approvals (IRB where applicable)
- Define target population, inclusion/exclusion criteria
- Validate on external datasets and prospective data (if available)
- Establish clinician-in-the-loop review process

## Phase 4: Limitations and safety work

- Bias/fairness analysis across skin tones and imaging conditions
- Clear UI/UX disclaimers and safe messaging
- Audit trails and monitoring (if deployed in controlled research settings)
