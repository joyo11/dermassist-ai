# Disclaimer (Required)

DermAssist AI is an **AI-assisted early skin lesion risk screening research prototype**.

## Not medical advice / not a diagnosis

- This tool **does not diagnose** skin cancer (or any condition).
- Outputs are **educational and research** results only.
- Do **not** use the results to make medical decisions.
- Always consult a licensed clinician for medical concerns.

## Data and model limitations

- Performance may vary across skin tones, lighting conditions, camera quality, and lesion types.
- Public datasets may not represent real-world clinical diversity.
- False negatives and false positives are possible.

## Intended use

DermAssist AI is intended to support **research exploration** of computer vision models and user-facing risk screening UX—**not** clinical deployment.
